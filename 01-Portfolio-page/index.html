About Me

Med Student & Developer

I am a medical student studying at Ankara University Faculty of Medicine. I am currently working as an assistant researcher in the Turkey section of a European Union funded project on research ethics. I am also the co-founder of a company with the theme of 'serious games', which we plan to bring a new understanding of education to the table.
I have been working on front-end developing for a while. I can provide clean code and pixel perfect design. I also make the website more & more interactive with web animations. A responsive design make your website accessible to all users, regardless of their device.
